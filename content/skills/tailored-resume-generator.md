## What This Skill Does

Analyzes job descriptions and generates tailored resumes that highlight relevant experience, skills, and achievements to maximize interview chances.

## Resume Structure Guidelines

| Section | Guidelines |
|---------|------------|
| **Professional Summary** | 3-4 lines, lead with years of experience, include top 3-4 required skills |
| **Technical Skills** | Group by category, list required tools first, use exact job description terminology |
| **Professional Experience** | Reverse chronological, emphasize relevant achievements, quantify with metrics |
| **Education** | Degrees relevant to position, certifications that match requirements |
| **Optional** | Certifications, Publications, Awards, Volunteer Work, Projects |

## ATS Optimization Tips

| Do | Don't |
|----|-------|
| Use standard section headings | Use tables, graphics, headers/footers |
| Incorporate exact keywords naturally | Include personal info (age, marital status, photo) |
| Include acronyms and full terms | Use first-person pronouns (I, me, my) |
| Match job title terminology where truthful | List references ("available upon request") |
| Keep to 1-2 pages max | Use generic templates without customization |

## Best Practices

### Do
- Be truthful and accurate — never fabricate experience
- Use industry-standard terminology
- Quantify achievements with specific metrics
- Tailor each resume to specific job
- Proofread for grammar and consistency
- Keep language concise and impactful

### Don't
- Include personal information unless requested
- Use first-person pronouns
- Include references
- List every job if career is 20+ years
- Use generic templates without customization
- Exceed 2 pages unless very senior role

## Special Considerations

| Situation | Approach |
|-----------|----------|
| **Career Changers** | Use functional/hybrid format, emphasize transferable skills |
| **Recent Graduates** | Lead with education, include coursework, projects, internships |
| **Senior Executives** | Lead with executive summary, focus on leadership and strategic impact |
| **Technical Roles** | Include technical skills section prominently, list languages/frameworks/tools |
