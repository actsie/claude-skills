# Marketing Skills Pack

33 specialized AI skills for SaaS marketing, built by Corey Haines. Covers the full marketing stack from top-of-funnel content to conversion optimization, email sequences, paid ads, and revenue operations.

<Callout type="tip">
Each skill is focused on a specific marketing task — not generic "help me with marketing" prompts. These are opinionated, tactical skills built for people who run growth at SaaS companies.
</Callout>

## Skill Library

<Card title="Content & SEO">

- **`ai-seo`** — Optimize content for AI search engines and LLM citations (GEO)
- **`content-strategy`** — Plan content strategy, decide what topics to cover
- **`competitor-alternatives`** — Create competitor comparison pages for SEO
- **`programmatic-seo`** — Build SEO-driven pages at scale using templates and data
- **`schema-markup`** — Add and optimize structured data markup
- **`seo-audit`** — Audit and diagnose SEO issues
- **`site-architecture`** — Plan and restructure page hierarchy and URL structure
- **`free-tool-strategy`** — Plan and evaluate free tools for lead gen and SEO

</Card>

<Card title="Copywriting & Creative">

- **`copywriting`** — Write marketing copy for homepages and landing pages
- **`copy-editing`** — Edit and improve existing marketing copy
- **`ad-creative`** — Generate and iterate ad headlines and descriptions
- **`social-content`** — Create and optimize social media content
- **`marketing-psychology`** — Apply behavioral science principles to marketing

</Card>

<Card title="Conversion Rate Optimization">

- **`page-cro`** — Optimize marketing pages for conversions
- **`signup-flow-cro`** — Optimize signup and trial activation flows
- **`onboarding-cro`** — Optimize post-signup onboarding and activation
- **`form-cro`** — Optimize lead capture and contact forms
- **`popup-cro`** — Create and optimize popups, modals, and overlays
- **`paywall-upgrade-cro`** — Create and optimize in-app paywalls and upsells

</Card>

<Card title="Email & Lifecycle">

- **`email-sequence`** — Create and optimize drip campaigns and lifecycle flows
- **`cold-email`** — Write B2B cold emails and follow-up sequences
- **`lead-magnets`** — Create and optimize lead magnets for email capture
- **`churn-prevention`** — Build cancellation flows and recover failed payments

</Card>

<Card title="Paid Acquisition">

- **`paid-ads`** — Manage campaigns across Google, Meta, LinkedIn, Twitter/X
- **`ab-test-setup`** — Plan and implement A/B tests and experiments
- **`analytics-tracking`** — Set up and audit analytics tracking

</Card>

<Card title="Strategy & Operations">

- **`pricing-strategy`** — Pricing decisions, packaging, and monetization
- **`launch-strategy`** — Plan product launches and feature announcements
- **`marketing-ideas`** — Generate marketing strategies for SaaS products
- **`product-marketing-context`** — Create product marketing context documents
- **`referral-program`** — Create and optimize referral and affiliate programs
- **`sales-enablement`** — Create pitch decks and objection handling docs
- **`revops`** — Manage revenue ops and marketing-to-sales handoff

</Card>
